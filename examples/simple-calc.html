Simple Beam Calculation

A quick check of a steel beam.

\[ \begin{alignedat}{2} L &= 6 \text{ m} \\\\ w &= 21000 \text{ kg s}^{-2} \\\\ f_y &= 300000000 \text{ Pa} \\\\ S_x &= 8.1E-07 \text{ m}^{3} \\\\ M ^ * &= \frac{w L^{2}}{8} \\ &= \frac{21 \times 10^{3} \text{ kg s}^{-2} \times \left(6 \text{ m}\right)^{2}}{8} \\ &= 94.5 \times 10^{3} \text{ N m} \\\\ M_s &= f_y S_x \\ &= 300 \text{ MPa} \times 810 \text{ mm}^{3} \\ &= 243 \text{ N m} \\\\ \eta &= \frac{M ^ *}{M_s} \\ &= \frac{94.5 \times 10^{3} \text{ N m}}{243 \text{ N m}} \\ &= 388.9 \\ \end{alignedat} \]
