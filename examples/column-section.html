Steel Column Section Properties

This calculation determines the section properties of a rectangular hollow section (RHS) steel column.

Calculations

\[ \begin{alignedat}{2} D &= 200 \text{ mm} \\\\ B &= 100 \text{ mm} \\\\ t &= 6 \text{ mm} \\\\ d &= D - 2 t \\ &= 200 \text{ mm} - 2 \times 6 \text{ mm} \\ &= 188 \text{ mm} \\\\ b &= B - 2 t \\ &= 100 \text{ mm} - 2 \times 6 \text{ mm} \\ &= 88 \text{ mm} \\\\ A_g &= D B - d b \\ &= 200 \text{ mm} \times 100 \text{ mm} - 188 \text{ mm} \times 88 \text{ mm} \\ &= 3,456 \text{ mm}^{2} \\\\ I_x &= \frac{B D^{3} - b d^{3}}{12} \\ &= \frac{100 \text{ mm} \times \left(200 \text{ mm}\right)^{3} - 88 \text{ mm} \times \left(188 \text{ mm}\right)^{3}}{12} \\ &= 17.94 \times 10^{-6} \text{ m}^{4} \\\\ I_y &= \frac{D B^{3} - d b^{3}}{12} \\ &= \frac{200 \text{ mm} \times \left(100 \text{ mm}\right)^{3} - 188 \text{ mm} \times \left(88 \text{ mm}\right)^{3}}{12} \\ &= 5.99 \times 10^{6} \text{ mm}^{4} \\\\ Z_x &= \frac{I_x}{\frac{D}{2}} \\ &= \frac{17.94 \times 10^{-6} \text{ m}^{4}}{\frac{200 \text{ mm}}{2}} \\ &= 179.4 \times 10^{3} \text{ mm}^{3} \\\\ Z_y &= \frac{I_y}{\frac{B}{2}} \\ &= \frac{5.99 \times 10^{6} \text{ mm}^{4}}{\frac{100 \text{ mm}}{2}} \\ &= 119.8 \times 10^{3} \text{ mm}^{3} \\ \end{alignedat} \]

Summary

The 200 x 100 x 6 RHS section has the following calculated properties:

  • Gross Area (A_g)
  • Second Moment of Area about major axis (I_x) and minor axis (I_y)
  • Section Modulus (Z_x, Z_y)
